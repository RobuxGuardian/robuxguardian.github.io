RobuxGuardian

Keeping You Safe from Roblox Scams

What is RobuxGuardian?

RobuxGuardian is a browser extension designed to protect Roblox players from online scams, phishing websites, and fake 'free Robux' offers. We aim to create a safe online experience for everyone, especially minors, in the Roblox community.

Key Features

  • Block Scam Websites: Automatically blocks access to known scam sites offering fake 'free Robux'.
  • Real-Time Alerts: Receive immediate warnings when visiting suspicious sites.
  • Constant Updates: Regularly updated blacklist to keep up with emerging threats.

How It Works

Once installed, RobuxGuardian works quietly in the background. It automatically detects when a suspicious or scam website is being accessed, alerts you, and blocks the site to keep you safe.
