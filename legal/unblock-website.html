Website Unblocking Policy

This policy outlines the procedures and guidelines for requesting the unblocking of websites that have been mistakenly blacklisted by RobuxGuardian. We aim to ensure a safe online environment for the Roblox community while allowing legitimate websites access.

1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide clear guidelines for users who believe that a website has been unjustly blacklisted, allowing them to request a review for unblocking.

2. Eligibility for Unblocking

Requests for unblocking will be considered under the following conditions:

  • The website must provide legitimate services or content.
  • The website should not be involved in any scams, phishing attempts, or activities targeting minors.
  • The requestor must provide sufficient evidence supporting their claim for unblocking.

3. Submission Process

To request the unblocking of a website, users must complete the Website Unblocking Request Form, providing the following information:

  • Website URL: The full address of the website you wish to unblock.
  • Reason for Unblocking: A brief explanation of why the website should be unblocked.
  • Your Contact Information: Your first name and email address for communication regarding your request.
  • Additional Information (Optional): Any other details that may help in the evaluation.

4. Review Process

Once submitted, the request will be reviewed by our team. We may contact the requestor for additional information if needed. The evaluation process typically takes 5-7 business days.

5. Decision Notification

After the review, the requestor will be notified of the decision via email. If the request is approved, the website will be removed from the blacklist. If denied, the requestor will receive an explanation for the decision.

6. Policy Updates

RobuxGuardian reserves the right to update this policy as needed. Users will be notified of any significant changes to the unblocking procedures.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this policy or the unblocking process, please contact us at: info.robuxguardian@gmail.com.

Thank you for your cooperation in helping us maintain a safer online environment for the Roblox community!
