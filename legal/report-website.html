Website Reporting Agreement

By submitting a report through our Report Form, you agree to the following terms:

1. Purpose of Reporting

This agreement outlines the procedures for reporting websites suspected of engaging in malicious or deceptive practices. Our goal is to maintain a safe environment for users within the Roblox community.

2. Information Submission

You agree to provide accurate and complete information regarding the website you are reporting, including:

  • The URL of the website
  • A detailed description of the issue
  • Any supporting evidence, if available

3. Confidentiality

All information provided in your report will be treated confidentially. Your data will be handled according to our privacy policy and will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

4. Review Process

Your report will be reviewed by our team. If the website is found to be involved in malicious or suspicious activity, it will be added to the RobuxGuardian blacklist.

5. Communication

You may be contacted by our support staff for more details regarding your report. If you provide your contact information, we may reach out to you directly.

6. Case Number

You will receive a unique case number for your report via email. Please keep this number safe, as it relates to your submission in our database.

7. Acceptance of Terms

By submitting your report, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Website Reporting Agreement, and you accept these terms.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this agreement or the reporting process, please contact us at info.robuxguardian@gmail.com.
